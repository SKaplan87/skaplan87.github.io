Qwikist

Built with Ruby on Rails, HTML, JavaScript, Materialize, and Devise

A tool for personal Trainers to run their business. Track the gyms you use and the clients you work with. How many sessions have you done, how much have you made, even automatically calculate the taxes you will owe! An easy to add note system on each client’s page makes it easy to track their progress to help them achieve their goals!

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Soccer Shootout

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

A 2 player game that pits the ninja turtles vs. Shredder and the Foot. Click and hold the accuracy button to choose how far right your shot will go. Click and hold the power button to choose how far up your shot will go. The player with the highest score at the end of 5 rounds wins!

Hangman

Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Electron

The classic game of Hangman. Try to guess the word of phrase before your character meets his demise. The power of electron allows the game to be downloaded as a desktop application. No need for the internet once you have the game! We take hangman very seriously, so no puzzles have any hints!

Planda Planning App

Built with Ruby on Rails, HTML, JavaScript, Materialize, and Devise

A planning app that allows you to create tasks for different teams. You can view all tasks for you across all of your teams, or view all of the tasks for a specific team. Track what tasks have been completed, and how much is left to do of those that are not yet done. A spotify iframe allows you to listen to music as you work on your task. A giphy API and random compliment generator are included to make even the boring tasks a little more fun!
